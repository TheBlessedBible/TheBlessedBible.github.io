In the Beginning

Next

Long-time ago, before everything began, there were no landforms and water forms, there was nothing to see, but darkness. In the middle of the darkness, God was there and commanded light. So, the very first day began.

God started to create the seas. God created the land in the middle of the seas, covering it with different kinds of trees and plants

Above this, God placed a shining sun by day, and a shimmering moon by night. He added billions of twinkling stars in the sky.

After creating the land and seas, God put fish of different colors in the seas, and he added different kinds of sea creatures like creeping crabs, sly sharks, and huge wallowing whales.

Above the land, God created birds, Beautiful butterflies, busy bees, and bustling bats that filled the air.
On the land, he created different kinds of animals, these animals can gallop, trot, lope, or hop. 

Then God created Adam and Eve, the first man and woman in the World. God trusted them to take care of his creation. Everything was happy and contented. God created the world in six days, and on the seventh day, God decided to declare it as the rest day, the Sabbath.
